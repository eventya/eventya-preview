Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Summary: Eventya is a data processor acting on behalf of DMO customers (data controllers). We collect minimal data, store everything in the EU (Google Cloud Frankfurt), never sell personal data, and comply fully with GDPR. Read the full policy below for details.

1. Who We Are

Eventya (operated by Digital Neighbourhood SRL, a company incorporated in Romania, CUI [←insert CUI], registered at [←insert address]) is the provider of the Eventya Destination Management Platform, accessible at eventya.net and related subdomains.

For the purposes of the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679), Eventya acts as a data processor when processing personal data on behalf of our customers (Destination Management Organisations and other entities, who are the data controllers), and as a data controller in its own right when processing data related to our own business operations (e.g. prospective customers, newsletter subscribers, support contacts).

Contact: privacy@eventya.net | hello@eventya.net

2. Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Data you provide directly

  • Account registration: name, email address, organisation name, country. Used to create and manage your Eventya account.
  • Billing information: email, billing address, VAT number. Payment card data is processed directly by our payment provider (Stripe) and never stored on Eventya servers.
  • Support requests: email address and message content when you contact us via the Help form or email.
  • Content you upload: texts, images, and other materials you publish through the CMS. This content may contain personal data (e.g. photos of staff members).

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration, error logs. Used to improve the platform and troubleshoot issues.
  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type, operating system, screen resolution. Used for security, fraud prevention and platform compatibility.
  • Cookies: see our Cookie Policy for full details.

2.3 End-visitor data (processed on behalf of our customers)

When your destination platform is live, end-visitors may interact with your website, mobile app, AI agent or WhatsApp channel. Any personal data collected from end-visitors (e.g. email addresses submitted via forms, WhatsApp phone numbers, analytics identifiers) is processed by Eventya as a data processor acting on your instructions as the data controller. Our Data Processing Agreement (DPA) governs this relationship.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained for the duration of the contract and deleted within 90 days of account closure, unless legal obligations require longer retention.
  • Billing records: retained for 10 years in accordance with Romanian and EU accounting law.
  • Support communications: retained for 3 years.
  • Usage logs and analytics: retained for 13 months, then aggregated or deleted.
  • End-visitor data: retention periods are set by the data controller (our customer) and specified in the DPA.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18): Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@eventya.net. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Romanian Data Protection Authority (ANSPDCP) at dataprotection.ro or with the supervisory authority in your country of residence.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures with notification timelines compliant with GDPR Art. 33
  • Employee training on data protection

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Children's Data

The Eventya platform is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us immediately at privacy@eventya.net.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
