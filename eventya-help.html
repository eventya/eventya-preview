FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

🖥️

Website & CMS

How do I add a new attraction?

Go to Content → Attractions in your back-office, click Add Attraction, fill in the details and hit Publish. It will appear on your website, mobile app and AI agent instantly.

Can I manage content in multiple languages?

Yes — Eventya supports up to 6 languages per platform. Each content item has a language tab. You can translate manually or use the built-in AI translation assistant.

How do I create a themed route or itinerary?

Go to Content → Routes, create a new route, then drag and drop attractions into the order you want. Routes appear on the website, app and can be shared via WhatsApp.

Can I embed my platform on an existing website?

Yes — you can embed specific views (map, event list, attraction detail) as iframes, or use the Eventya API to pull content into an existing site.
📱

Mobile App

Is the mobile app included in my plan?

Yes — every Eventya plan includes a native iOS and Android app. It is automatically generated from your CMS content and stays in sync.

How do visitors download the app?

Your app is published to the App Store and Google Play under your destination's name. You get a direct download link and QR code for print materials.

Does the app work offline?

Yes — attractions, routes and maps are cached for offline use. Visitors can navigate even without a data connection.

How often is the app updated when I change content?

Content changes sync to the app in real time via push. No app store update is needed for content changes — only for feature or design updates.
🤖

AI Agent & WhatsApp

What can the AI agent answer?

The AI answers questions about your attractions, opening hours, routes, events, and practical visitor info — all based on your CMS content. It does not hallucinate outside your data.

How do I connect WhatsApp?

Go to Channels → WhatsApp, connect your Twilio account, and activate a WhatsApp Business number. Setup takes about 15 minutes.

Can the AI hand off to a human agent?

Yes — if the AI cannot answer, it can escalate the conversation to your helpdesk team via the Eventya Visitor Helpdesk module.

Is the AI available 24/7?

Yes — the AI agent and WhatsApp bot are always on. Your helpdesk team handles escalations during your defined working hours.
💳

Billing & Pricing

How much does Eventya cost?

Eventya starts at €0 — you get a fully functional platform on a test URL for free. You pay only when you switch to your own custom domain. Paid plans start at €170/month.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as bank transfer for annual plans. Invoicing in EUR.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes — you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel anytime from your account settings. No minimum contract, no cancellation fees.
⏳

When do I pay?

When does my free period start?

Your free period starts when you create your platform. There is no time limit — you stay free for as long as you are on the test URL.

What triggers the first payment?

The first invoice is generated when you add your custom domain and activate it. Until then, everything is completely free.

Is there a trial period on paid plans?

Every paid plan includes a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied in the first 30 days after going live, we refund in full.
📊

Limits of usage

How many attractions can I add?

There is no hard limit on attractions. All plans support unlimited content items — attractions, events, routes, accommodations and more.

Are there bandwidth or visitor limits?

No bandwidth caps. For AI and WhatsApp, monthly message limits apply per plan. Check the Pricing page for details per tier.

How many team members can access the back-office?

Starter plans include 3 users. Growth and Enterprise plans include unlimited users with role-based permissions.
🌱

Free for Non-profits & Public DMOs

Is Eventya free for non-profit organisations?

We offer a 50% discount for registered non-profit organisations and public DMOs. Contact us with your registration details and we will set up the discount on your account.

Can EU-funded projects use Eventya?

Yes — Eventya has been used on EU PNRR-funded projects including România Atractivă (€7.4M). We can provide the necessary documentation for public procurement processes.

Do you offer special pricing for small local DMOs?

Yes — reach out via the form below and describe your organisation. We evaluate each case individually and have flexible arrangements for small destinations with limited budgets.
