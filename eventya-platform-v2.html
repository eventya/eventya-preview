Frequently asked

Everything DMO managers ask before going live.

How long does it take to go live?

Platform created in under 10 seconds. First content added and live on test URL in under 10 minutes. Live on your custom domain including DNS setup in about 30 minutes. No technical team needed.

Do I need developers or a technical team?

No. Eventya is designed for destination managers, not developers. The only technical step is pointing your domain's DNS — something your IT team can do in 5 minutes.

Where is our data stored? Is it GDPR compliant?

All data is stored in EU data centers (Frankfurt, Google Cloud). We never transfer visitor data outside the EEA. Full GDPR compliance out of the box — including cookie consent manager and a standard DPA for all customers.

Which languages are supported?

The platform interface supports 20+ languages. Content is managed per language in the CMS. Standard plans include 6 languages. The AI agent automatically detects and responds in the visitor's language regardless of your published languages.

Can we migrate from our existing platform?

Yes. CSV/JSON import tool for attractions, events and routes. Enterprise customers get direct migration support. Run Eventya in parallel before switching DNS — zero downtime migration.

Is there an API for integrating with our systems?

Yes. REST API for content and webhook system for helpdesk events. Enterprise customers get full API access including custom integrations with regional tourism databases, ticketing systems or CRMs.
