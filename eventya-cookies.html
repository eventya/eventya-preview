Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Summary: We use strictly necessary cookies (required for the platform to work), optional analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4), and optional marketing cookies (Meta Pixel — disabled by default). You can manage your preferences at any time.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — such as your language preference or login session. Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, local storage and session storage.

This Cookie Policy applies to the Eventya platform (eventya.net and all customer destination platforms powered by Eventya) and should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

2. Who Sets Cookies

First-party cookies are set by Eventya directly. Third-party cookies are set by services we integrate (Google, Meta, etc.). Each category is described below.

If you are visiting a destination platform powered by Eventya (e.g. a city tourism website), the operator of that platform is the data controller for cookies set on that platform and should publish their own cookie notice. Eventya provides the technology and acts as data processor.

4. Your Choices

When you first visit the Eventya platform, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject optional cookies, or customise your preferences by category.

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie settings" in the footer of any page. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the platform from working correctly.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. We honour this signal by not setting optional analytics or marketing cookies when it is detected, without requiring you to interact with the cookie banner.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add or remove cookies or change how we use them. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
